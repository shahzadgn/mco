Google Ads Roadmap

Paid Search Execution Plan

Month 1: The Fix

Weeks 1-4
  • Conversion AuditMove "PDF Downloads" to Secondary. Keep only "Book Demo".
  • URL HygieneAudit Final URLs for 404s. Implement 301 redirects.
  • Spend StabilizationNormalize daily budgets to prevent volatility.

Month 2: The Refine

Weeks 5-8
  • Match Type ConstrictionMove "Regulatory Change" keywords to Phrase/Exact match.

Month 3: The Scale
